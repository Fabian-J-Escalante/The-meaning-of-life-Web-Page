About the meaning of life
Like the ocean life has waves

We are swimming through an open sea of endlessness, we can ride a wave or the waves can crash on us and sink us, the same can be said to the points of our lives, I’m referring to those low points, the rock bottom, and the higher and most successful points of our lives, we all are going to experience the two ends of the moments in life and that’s fine, of course we can that-is-life-ing through the rest of our lives but we must make the best out of the worst times and appreciate the good ones.

The thing is that, were we are right now, contributes to our view on life, we must remember that the waves of life are temporal they will come and after some time they will go away, the low moments eventually become better and the high moments eventually become worse. Sometimes things happen in life that can make you the happiest person on the world or crush you and take away the identity you had about yourself, these moments are important for the crafting of the meaning you are going to give to life, each point is a different brush and instrument to draw and paint in the canvas of life.

After you ride some waves and get sunken by other ones you’ll eventually find some common ground on your views about life, and I mean that plurally, because as life flows your view of life is prone to change but, at the end, you will combine these views and make a new one that is right for you and for the experiences you had in life, although even this new view may change a little, so we must keep swimming, each wave we pass make us stronger and the swimming part becomes easier.
